## Core Result

RGAP demonstrates that:

- unconstrained optimization may minimize residual while becoming structurally invalid,
- ambient physical feasibility is not equivalent to observational admissibility,
- admissibility-preserving transport may intentionally accept higher residual misfit.

## Scope Boundary

This release is a bounded computational demonstration of admissibility-constrained inference under degraded observability.

It is not:

- an operational search method,
- a reconstruction engine,
- a predictive aviation system,
- or a claim regarding MH370 recovery.
